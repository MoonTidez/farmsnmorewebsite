What is Farms N' More?

Farms N' More is a group of lazy people who have come together to develop Hypixel Skyblock Farms that work in a unique way. These farms allow players to farm for hours on hours without having to change their player direction. This basically allows them to farm without even having to touch their mouse, and all you need to do is hold down left click (or any other button you bind your break key to), your left or right strafe key and in most farms your forward key as well.
